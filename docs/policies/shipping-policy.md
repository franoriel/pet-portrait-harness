# Shipping Policy

**Effective date:** [DATE_EFFECTIVE]

Pet Printables ships through our print partner **Printful**, which operates fulfilment centres in the United States and Europe. Every portrait is printed to order, so total delivery time = **production time + transit time**.

## 1. Where we ship

- **Canada** — all provinces and territories.
- **United States** — all 50 states.
- **International** — currently **not available**; please email [SUPPORT_EMAIL] if you'd like to be notified when we add your country.

Shipping destinations outside Canada may be subject to import duties, taxes, or customs fees levied by the destination country. These are the recipient's responsibility and are not included in our prices.

## 2. Processing time

After you approve a preview and complete checkout, your order typically enters production within **1–2 hours** and is produced in **2–5 business days**. This is the time it takes to print, frame (if applicable), quality-check, and pack your portrait.

Orders placed on weekends or statutory holidays begin production on the next business day.

## 3. Transit time (once shipped)

| Destination | Typical transit | Carrier |
|---|---|---|
| Canada (BC, AB) | 3–5 business days | Canada Post / UPS |
| Canada (other provinces) | 5–8 business days | Canada Post / UPS |
| United States | 4–7 business days | USPS / UPS |

Total delivery time is typically **6–13 business days** from order to doorstep. Framed canvas may take 1–3 additional days due to framing and extra packaging.

## 4. Shipping rates

- **Standard shipping** — a flat rate is calculated at checkout based on your destination and parcel weight.
- **Free shipping** on Canadian orders **$85 CAD or more** (subtotal before taxes).
- We do not currently offer expedited or overnight shipping.

The exact rate is shown before you confirm payment.

## 5. Tracking

A tracking number is emailed as soon as Printful releases the parcel to the carrier (typically within 48 hours of order confirmation). You can also view tracking from your Shopify order-status page.

## 6. Delays we don't control

Once a parcel has left Printful's facility, delivery timing is in the carrier's hands. Weather events, customs holds, strikes, and postal backlogs during the holiday season can add delays. We monitor tracking and proactively reach out if a parcel stops moving for 7+ days.

## 7. Lost or missing parcels

If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you cannot find the parcel:

1. Check with building reception, neighbours, and your mailbox/lockbox.
2. Wait 48 hours — carriers sometimes mark parcels as delivered a day early.
3. If it still hasn't arrived, email [SUPPORT_EMAIL] with your order number.

If tracking has not updated in **7+ days**, we consider the parcel lost and will reprint and re-ship at our expense, or issue a full refund at your choice.

## 8. Wrong address

Please double-check your shipping address before checkout. If a parcel is returned to Printful because of an incorrect address, we can re-ship to a corrected address at **cost** (the re-shipping fee is passed on to you).

## 9. Damaged on arrival

If your parcel arrives damaged, please photograph the outer packaging before opening. Email [SUPPORT_EMAIL] with the order number and photos, and we will arrange a free replacement under our **Refund Policy**.
