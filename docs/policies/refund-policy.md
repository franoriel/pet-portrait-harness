# Return and Refund Policy

**Effective date:** [DATE_EFFECTIVE]

We want you to love your Pet Printables portrait. Because every portrait is **custom-made to order** from the photo and pet name you supply, standard "change of mind" returns are not available. That said, we stand behind the quality of what we ship — and your statutory rights under British Columbia's Sale of Goods Act and the federal Competition Act are not affected by anything in this policy.

## 1. What's always refundable or replaceable

We will **replace your order at no cost, or issue a full refund**, if:

- **The print arrives damaged** (bent canvas, cracked frame, scuffed surface, punctured packaging).
- **The print has a manufacturing defect** (banding, streaking, wrong colours, blurry image, mis-cut edge, mis-aligned frame).
- **The print does not match the preview you approved** at checkout (wrong size, wrong pet name, wrong style).
- **Your order does not arrive** within 30 days of the tracking number's most recent update, and the carrier has not confirmed delivery.

To claim, email **[SUPPORT_EMAIL]** within **30 days of delivery** (or 45 days from the order date if nothing arrived) with:

1. your order number,
2. a photo of the issue (for damage or defect claims) clearly showing the problem,
3. the packaging photo if the damage was visible on arrival.

We aim to approve or deny claims within **2 business days**. Approved claims are reprinted and shipped at our expense.

## 2. What is **not** refundable

Because portraits are personalized to your photo and your pet's name, we cannot accept returns or refunds for:

- Change of mind after you approved the preview and completed checkout.
- The artistic interpretation of the AI (see Section 5 of our Terms of Service — style, fur markings, background, or pose variations are a creative feature of generative AI, not a defect).
- Dissatisfaction with a photo you uploaded that was low-resolution, blurry, or partially obstructed — we show a warning if we detect a quality issue, but the final choice to proceed is yours.
- Incorrect shipping address entered by you at checkout. (We can re-ship at your cost if the parcel is returned to us.)
- A pet name misspelled by you at checkout. (We will offer a 40% discount on a reprint as a one-time courtesy.)

## 3. Cancellations before production

You may cancel an order for a **full refund** if it has not yet entered production. Orders typically enter production within **1–2 hours** of payment, so please email [SUPPORT_EMAIL] immediately if you need to cancel. Once a job has been sent to our print partner, it cannot be cancelled.

## 4. How refunds are issued

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method via Shopify Payments / Stripe. Processing time:

- **Credit / debit card:** 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.
- **Apple Pay / Google Pay / Shop Pay:** typically the next business day.
- **Gift card / store credit (if used):** immediately back on the card.

If you paid partly with a promotional discount, the discount is not refunded in cash, but the remaining paid amount is.

## 5. Shipping costs on returns

For damage, defect, or mismatch claims, **we cover all shipping costs** and you do not need to ship the defective item back — we'll ask for photos and destroy your end securely. Where we do request a return (e.g. a framed product with suspected carrier damage), we provide a prepaid Canada Post label.

## 6. Your statutory rights

Nothing in this policy limits your rights under:

- the **British Columbia Sale of Goods Act**, which implies conditions of merchantable quality and fitness for purpose,
- the **British Columbia Consumer Protection Act**,
- the federal **Competition Act**, or
- the consumer protection laws of your province of residence.

If a remedy in this policy is less favourable than a statutory remedy you're entitled to, the statutory remedy applies.
