# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** [DATE_EFFECTIVE]

Pet Printables ("we", "our", "us") is operated by [BUSINESS_LEGAL_NAME] and is based in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. This policy describes the personal information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have under Canada's **Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)** and British Columbia's **Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)**.

By using petprintables.ca or placing an order, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described here.

## 1. Information we collect

**Information you give us directly**

- Account and order details: name, shipping address, billing address, email, phone (optional).
- Photos you upload to generate pet portraits, and the pet names you enter.
- Payment information, which is handled by Shopify Payments / Stripe — we never see or store full card numbers.
- Communications you send to support.

**Information collected automatically**

- Device, browser, IP address, referrer, pages visited, time on page, and general location (from IP) via Shopify analytics.
- Cookies and similar technologies for cart state, session continuity, bot protection (Cloudflare Turnstile), and conversion tracking.

**Information we generate**

- AI-generated portrait images derived from your uploaded photo.
- Order-fulfilment records linking you to Printful shipments.
- Audit-trail records of your **photo-licence acceptance** (ISO-8601 timestamp + client IP) captured when you tick the consent box before generating.

## 2. How we use your information

- **Fulfil your order** — generate your portrait, run quality checks, print, ship, and provide customer support.
- **Payments** — process your purchase, detect fraud, and issue refunds where applicable.
- **Service operation** — keep the site running, prevent abuse (rate limiting, bot detection), and debug errors.
- **Legal obligations** — tax, accounting, and responding to lawful requests by Canadian authorities.
- **Marketing** — only with your **express consent** as required by Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). You can withdraw consent at any time using the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do not use your uploaded pet photos to train AI models.

## 4. How long we keep your information

- **Uploaded pet photos** — automatically deleted from our servers **30 days** after order completion. You can request earlier deletion by emailing [SUPPORT_EMAIL].
- **Generated portraits** — stored in your browser's local storage for **24 hours** (the "Your Portraits" library). Our CDN retains print-ready copies for **90 days** after order completion for reprint/complaint purposes.
- **Order records** — kept for **7 years** as required by Canadian tax law.
- **Marketing list entries** — kept until you unsubscribe.
- **Photo-licence audit logs** — kept for **2 years** to defend against potential copyright disputes.

## 5. Your rights

Under PIPEDA / PIPA you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate information.
- **Withdraw consent** for any processing that is not legally required, including marketing.
- **Request deletion** of your uploaded photos and account (note: we must keep order records for tax purposes).
- **Complain** to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at priv.gc.ca if you believe we've mishandled your information.

To exercise any right, email [SUPPORT_EMAIL]. We will respond within **30 days**.

## 6. Security

- All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).
- Payments run through PCI-compliant Shopify Payments; we never store card data.
- Uploaded photos are stored in Cloudflare R2 with per-object URLs.
- We use Cloudflare Turnstile and rate limiting to block automated abuse.
- Access to customer data is restricted to the operator and audited.

No system is 100% secure. If we experience a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada as required by PIPEDA.

## 7. Cookies & tracking

We use essential cookies for cart, session, and bot protection. Analytics and marketing cookies only run if you accept them via the cookie banner. You can clear cookies at any time from your browser settings.

## 8. Children

Pet Printables is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has given us personal information, email [SUPPORT_EMAIL] and we will delete it.
